# Stakeholder Update Template

Use this format to provide clear and structured updates during production incidents or service disruptions.

## Initial Incident Notification

**Subject:** Incident Notification: [Application/Service Name] - [Brief Issue Summary]

Hi Team,

We are currently investigating an issue affecting **[application/service/process]**.

**Impact:**  
[Describe business/user impact clearly.]

**Start Time:**  
[Date and time]

**Current Status:**  
Investigation is in progress.

**Teams Engaged:**  
[List teams involved, for example Application Support, Platform, Infrastructure, Vendor, Business Team.]

**Next Update:**  
We will provide the next update by [time].

Regards,  
[Your Name]
